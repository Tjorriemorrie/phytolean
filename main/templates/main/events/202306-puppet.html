Food Theatre

A fun and informative puppet show about making healthy food choices.

Date: 20 June & 4, 11, 18 July 2023

Where: Tuesdays

Time: 11:00-12:00

Where: Schoemansville Library

Entry: Free Event

For: Kids ages 3-12 Years

Presentation in English

Join us for an Exciting Adventure!

Are you ready for a one-of-a-kind experience that combines fun, education, and deliciousness? Food Theatre is here to make learning about healthy food choices a blast for your kids!

What's in Store for You?

  • Puppet Show: Our lively puppet characters will take your children on a journey filled with laughter, learning, and valuable lessons about making healthy food choices.
  • Song and Dance: Get ready to sing and dance along! Bring energy and excitement to the stage, ensuring an engaging experience for every child.
  • Power Foods: Discover the secrets of power foods that build powerful bodies! Through interactive activities and visuals, your kids will learn about the amazing benefits of nutritious foods.
  • The Powerplate: Step into the world of the Powerplate, where each superfood's color and superpower will be revealed. Your child will be amazed by the magic and wonders of nature's vibrant palette.
  • Eat the Rainbow: Find out why we should eat the rainbow! Our experts will explain the importance of incorporating a variety of colorful fruits and vegetables into our diets for optimal health.
  • Colour-In Activity: Unleash your child's creativity with a color-in activity. They will have the chance to showcase their artistic skills while reinforcing the concepts they've learned during the show.

Unlock the Superhero Within!

Food Theatre aims to inspire and empower your kids to become superheroes of their own health. By attending this hour-long activity, they will gain valuable knowledge, have loads of fun, and embark on a journey towards a healthier lifestyle.

Don't miss out on this fantastic opportunity for your children to learn, laugh, and discover the magic of nutritious food choices.
