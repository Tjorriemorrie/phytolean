Meet our Inspired Founder

Nerine is the managing Director for BSF Health Solutions and the creator of the acclaimed PhytoLean weight loss consultancy. With over fifteen years of experience working in the health and wellness industry, she loves sharing her passion for living a balanced and healthy lifestyle. She has collaborated with higher educational institutions, experts in their respective fields and associations to bring you the best nutritional information globally available.

Nerine holds a Bachelors in Health Sciences (Naturopathy), A Masters in Psychology with a focus on behavioural change psychology and eating psychology, and is currently completing her Ph.D. in Natural Medicine. She has lectured at university level and presented as a guest speaker at the acclaimed International ACRE conference. She is a certified clinical weight loss specialist accredited through the complementary medical association and registered with the ASCHP as Holistic Councillor, and with the IAHTNP as a registered naturopathic practitioner. In addition, Nerine is also a qualified educator and registered SACE health educator, assessor and moderator.

She holds advanced certificates in integrative nutrition, lifestyle medicine, culinary medicine, original and biblical traditional medicinal approaches and ethnomedicine practices. She is also a licensed Food For Life (FFL) instructor with the Physicians Committee for Responsible Medicine (PCRM) and proudly the first FFL instructor in South Africa.

Nerine presents the Food for Life award-winning nutrition education and cooking class program in partnership with the Physicians Committee for Responsible Medicine that provides an innovative approach to diet-related chronic diseases. Since 2001, Food for Life has been a pioneer in delivering hands-on information about the direct role of plant-based nutrition in health and disease prevention to communities around the world. Designed by physicians, nurses, and registered dietitians, Food for Life promotes healthful eating based on the latest scientific research.
