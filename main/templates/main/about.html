What’s in the name PhytoLean?

The healing Power of Nature- Boost your Immunity, slim down, protect your health, promote anti-aging and longevity.

This means these powerful plant particles have ani-aging and longevity properties. They influence and regulate hormonal functions, and regulated hormones play a huge role in normal weight maintenance. Furthermore, they boost immune function, helping fight bacteria, viruses and other pathogens. It is no wonder that diets that contain more of these powerful phyto-nutrients have demonstrated scientifically in studies that they have immense benefits in illness prevention, duration of illness and severity. Boosting your immunity is fundamental today in our global situation and can prove valuable and even essential in fighting off disease and disease prevention. These powerful plant compounds can lower blood clotting and coagulation proving very valuable in our day and age.

These wonderful particles from Nature’s medicine pharmacy most importantly also inhibit fat synthesis and storage! For this reason, making use of these powerful plant “phyto” chemicals to get lean and stay lean was the inspiration behind the name of our weight loss programme and product line “PhytoLean”.

Here at PhytoLean we believe in optimal body balance through Nature’s synergy, harnessing the power of plants. Powerful science working WITH Nature provides a gentle yet effective natural and sustainable weight loss solution for all.
